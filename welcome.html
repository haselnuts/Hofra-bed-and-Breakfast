Welcome to Hofra Bed & Breakfast!

Welcome to an accommodation beyond the usual in the beautiful countryside Hälsingland. Enjoy our hiking trails in the deep woodlands and the blue mountains, beautiful farming landscapes, the glittering sea close by and a fantastic culture to discover.

We are a small Bed & Breakfast and offer 4 personally decorated rooms with a total of 9 beds. The rooms are decorated in a harmonic style with history close by.

Hofra Bed & Breakfast offers a scenic living in a charming garden with peaceful surroundings. The beautiful garden invites you to enjoy a moment of serenity alone, with family or friends. Take your breakfast out in the beautiful garden or just enjoy a hot cup of coffee or tea in the sun.

Come and enjoy a moment of Hälsingland!
